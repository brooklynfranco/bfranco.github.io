About Us

4th & 10 Apparel is family owned and operated out of Syracuse, UT. We were tired of waiting for a printing company to create quality team apparel and make our experience personal. So, we decided to do it ourselves. At 4th & 10 Apparel, we try our best to make it a seamless process. We pride ourselves on creating quality products, personal customer service and reasonable turn-around times. Most of all, we want our customers to be able to represent their teams with pride.

Our Name

This dream for a local t-shirt printing company was established long before 2020. Our founder, Art Franco, grew up in Salt Lake City on 4th and 10.
